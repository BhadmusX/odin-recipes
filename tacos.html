Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells (soft or hard)
  • Ground beef or shredded chicken
  • Refried beans
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa or pico de gallo
  • Chopped onions
  • Chopped cilantro
  1. Cook the ground beef or shredded chicken in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave according to package instructions.
  3. Spread a layer of refried beans on each taco shell.
  4. Add the cooked meat on top of the beans.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
  6. Drizzle with sour cream and salsa or pico de gallo.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped onions and cilantro for extra flavor.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tacos!
