Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetables.

Ingredent

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage to the skillet and cook until browned.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer cooked noodles, meat mixture, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in the dish.
  8. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with marinara sauce and mozzarella on top.
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
